# SEO Content Writing Guidelines

## Keyword Density

### Primary Keyword
- Target density: **1–2%** of total word count
- Formula: `(primary_keyword_count / total_words) * 100`
- Example: 2000-word article → 20–40 occurrences of the primary keyword (counting partial phrase matches)

### Secondary / LSI Keywords
- Target density: **0.5–1%** each for top 3–5 LSI keywords
- Do not force all LSI terms — use those that fit naturally

### Density Violations
- Under 1% primary: keyword under-optimized, may not rank
- Over 2.5% primary: keyword stuffing risk, manual penalty possible
- Prioritize natural readability over hitting exact targets

## Readability Standards

### English (EN)
- **Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level: 8–10**
  - Grade 8: accessible to most adults, broad audience
  - Grade 10: suitable for educated audiences, technical topics
- Sentence length: average 15–20 words per sentence
- Paragraph length: 2–4 sentences per paragraph
- Avoid passive voice where possible (target < 15% passive sentences)
- Use contractions for conversational tone (don't, it's, you'll)

### Vietnamese (VI)
- Formal tone — no contractions equivalent
- Sentence length: **< 25 words average**
- Use Vietnamese alternatives over loan words where a natural equivalent exists
- Examples: use "phần mềm" not "software", "tải xuống" not "download", "trực tuyến" not "online" (when context allows)
- See `multilingual-rules.md` for full VI guidelines

## Article Length

| Content Type | Minimum | Maximum | Sweet Spot |
|--------------|---------|---------|------------|
| General blog | 1500 words | 3000 words | 2000–2500 |
| HowTo guide | 1500 words | 2500 words | 1800–2200 |
| FAQ page | 1200 words | 2000 words | 1500–1800 |
| Product review | 1500 words | 3000 words | 2000–2800 |

Never pad content to hit word count. Cut fluff; add value instead.

## Keyword Placement Rules

### Title (H1)
- Primary keyword must appear in the H1
- Place as close to the beginning as natural language allows
- Example: "SEO Keyword Research: Complete Guide for 2024" — not "A Complete 2024 Guide to SEO Keyword Research"

### First 100 Words (Introduction)
- Primary keyword must appear **at least once** within the first 100 words
- Ideal: within the first 50 words
- Do not force it into the very first sentence if it reads unnaturally

### H2 Headings
- Include primary keyword in **at least 2 H2 headings**
- Distribute LSI keywords across remaining H2s
- Do not repeat the exact same keyword phrase in multiple consecutive headings

### H3 Headings
- Use long-tail variations and LSI keywords
- Not required to include primary keyword — prioritize topical relevance

### Body Text
- Distribute keyword occurrences evenly — avoid front-loading or back-loading
- Bold the primary keyword on first occurrence only (optional, use sparingly)
- Use synonyms and semantic variations to avoid repetition

### Conclusion
- Include primary keyword once in the conclusion paragraph
- Pair with a clear CTA (call-to-action)

## Internal Linking Recommendations

- Include **2–5 internal links** per article
- Link to topically related content using descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Anchor text should include the target page's primary keyword where natural
- Place at least one internal link in the first half of the article
- Do not link to the same URL more than twice in one article

---

## Formatting Best Practices

- Use bullet lists for 3+ parallel items
- Use numbered lists only for sequential steps
- Use tables for comparisons (3+ items, 2+ attributes)
- Add a TL;DR or key takeaways box for articles over 2000 words
- Include at least one image alt text recommendation per 500 words
- Break up walls of text: no more than 4 consecutive paragraphs without a heading or list

## Tone and Voice

| Language | Tone | Voice | Contractions |
|----------|------|-------|--------------|
| EN | Conversational, helpful | Active, second-person ("you") | Yes (don't, you'll) |
| VI | Formal, respectful | Active where possible | No equivalent |

---

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any article:

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword density 1–2%
- [ ] At least 2 H2s include primary keyword or close variant
- [ ] No keyword stuffing in any single paragraph
- [ ] Readability grade 8–10 (EN) or avg sentence < 25 words (VI)
- [ ] Article length within target range (1500–3000 words)
- [ ] FAQ section present (if schema_type is FAQ or HowTo)
- [ ] Conclusion includes CTA
- [ ] 2–5 internal links with descriptive anchors
